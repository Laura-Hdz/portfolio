Fremmy

2018, digital (HTML, CS, JS / Atom, GitHub)

https://laura-hdz.github.io/dart450/final/fremmy/ (please watch in high quality!)

Fremmy was inspired by one of the hypothetical scenario presented by Pippin Barr in his Concordia University DART450 course: “Social media - one could imagine a webpage that extends on the idea of "social media" by extending it to a near-future vision of webpages/sites that serve as our friends in the absence of real world interactions with other humans.”

I created a website and experience that criticizes the use of social media and its interference in interpersonal relationships. Using basic jQuery, Fremmy enables and encourages the user to provide information about themselves through a “humanized” user-experience. It then uses the information gathered to create a profile for the user and “help" them connect with people who have similar interests or opinions. By leading and restricting the user's actions, Fremmy encourages a loss of agency. The name Fremmy came about because it felt fitting to use a catchy phonetic reference to the words friend and enemy.
