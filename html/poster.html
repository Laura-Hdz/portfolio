Commercial Type Poster

2016, digital, print (InDesign)

The mandate was to design a poster for a typography talk by Christian Schwartz at the Department of Design and Computation Arts in Concordia University (March, 2016).

This class project consisted of a 24" x 36" poster using Commercial Type foundry fonts and only two printing colours. The colour choice and layout were inspired by the Commercial Type website, which uses blue and black as its primary colours and the different typefaces are presented in a condensed, in-your-face manner. The title of the talk “It’s not just about the money, It’s not just about the art” is enhanced by the use of repetition while the type choice creates a texture that is visible even at a distance.
